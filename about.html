ABOUT ME

With over 14 years of experience, Lydia Tassara is a Costa Rican Professional Dancer, Movement Explorer, Teacher, and Choreographer specialized in Fusion Belly Dance.

Internationally certified from the top experts in the genre. In addition to focusing on dance, Lydia studies and practices various movement styles to enrich her artistic training, and she is a certified personal trainer in mobility programs. Her passion for dance drives her to continually research and explore, seeking new forms of expression and alternative methods to foster creativity and the infinite possibilities of movement.

Lydia travels to various countries, sharing her unique style at renowned festivals and teaching workshops. In Costa Rica, she leads her project, Aywa Fusion Tribe.

ABOUT AYWA

The fusion belly dance group "Aywa Fusion Tribe," lead by Lydia Tassara, is a multidisciplinary ensemble in constant evolution.

"Aywa" stands out for exploring creativity and integrating different choreographic styles, fostering unity among dancers of all levels.

This exciting space is the home of experimental dance, where the community constantly seeks innovation and artistic expression in weekly classes and. monthly workshops. To create unique choreographic pieces to perform in Costa Rica and at International well Dance Festivals.
